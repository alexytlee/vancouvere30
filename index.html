About the BMW E30

The BMW E30 generation (1982-1994) represents the essence of the "Ultimate Driving Machine" philosophy. With its perfect balance of performance, handling, and character, the E30 has become one of the most beloved BMWs ever produced.

Performance Heritage

From the iconic M3 to the reliable 325i, the E30 platform delivered exceptional driving dynamics that set the standard for sports sedans.

Enthusiast Friendly

Simple, well-engineered, and highly modifiable. The E30 is perfect for both daily driving and weekend track adventures.

Strong Community

Join a passionate community of owners who share knowledge, parts, and experiences to keep these classics on the road.
